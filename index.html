The House

Born of Royalty

Albert Roi is a fragrance house built on a singular conviction: that true luxury is not worn — it is commanded. Inspired by the regal spirit of kings and queens, each scent is crafted to embody power, poise, and presence. From the bold strength of the masculine line to the graceful elegance of the feminine, Albert Roi invites you into a world where every note tells a story of sovereignty.

Masculine Collection

Albert Roi

Masculine Variant

Forged in obsidian and gold, the Masculine Variant is a fragrance of unyielding authority. Its circular bottle — crowned with a sculpted cap of radiating gold — houses a scent as commanding as the emblem it bears: a sovereign figure, crowned and resolute. This is not a fragrance for the faint. It is for those who lead.

Fragrance Notes

  • Top — Black Pepper, Bergamot, Cardamom
  • Heart — Oud, Leather, Smoked Amber
  • Base — Patchouli, Vetiver, Dark Musk

Feminine Variant

Draped in ivory and gold, the Feminine Variant is elegance distilled. Its bottle — a luminous disc adorned with sunburst textures and a golden crown — holds a fragrance as graceful as the sovereign it celebrates. Embossed florals trace the packaging like whispered secrets. This is a scent for the woman who does not follow — she reigns.

Fragrance Notes

  • Top — Pear Blossom, Pink Pepper, Mandarin
  • Heart — Rose Absolute, Jasmine Sambac, Iris
  • Base — White Musk, Sandalwood, Vanilla Orchid
