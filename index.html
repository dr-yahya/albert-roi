The Albert Roi Perfume

Albert Roi is a luxury perfume house born from a vision of regal elegance and uncompromising craftsmanship. Each Albert Roi fragrance is composed to evoke strength, grace, and the quiet authority of royalty — a perfume experience designed to linger long after the first impression.

The Albert Roi perfume collection presents two distinct expressions of luxury: a masculine line in black and gold, and a feminine line in white and gold. Both share the same devotion to quality, presented as equal chapters in the Albert Roi fragrance story.

The masculine Albert Roi perfume commands attention with its black-and-gold presence. Built on a foundation of dark woods, amber, and subtle spice, this Albert Roi fragrance delivers depth and enduring strength — a scent for those who carry authority without effort.

Top notes
Bergamot, black pepper, cardamom
Heart notes
Cedarwood, leather, iris
Base notes
Amber, vetiver, musk

The feminine Albert Roi perfume radiates in white and gold. A luminous blend of white florals, soft musk, and golden amber, this Albert Roi fragrance captures grace and poise — an elegant perfume that unfolds with quiet confidence.

Top notes
White rose, pear blossom, saffron
Heart notes
Jasmine, tuberose, orange blossom
Base notes
White musk, sandalwood, vanilla
